Some Things About Me

Hi, I'm Your PHOTOGRAPHER STAMATIS.

Hi there , i am Stamatis,i am a photographer and videographer based in Elefsina. I grew up loving photography ,and i started from photographing the local games,and every time i got the chance to have the camera on me i was shooting something else. My main Work is Sports - Artistic photography - Portraits. I have been in the field for the past 5 years , and i know my way around the camera ! I mostly shoot sports like your local football game or the world championship in basket. I offer photos and video that will last a lifetime and never get of your mind.

I grew up in Athens - Greece , where I first fell in love with photography when i found my parents digicam ,when i was fiddling around the house.I started taking it everywhere and then it just stuck with me. Photography is something more than just capturing something, and this is something that anyone can see in my work. I don't just capture the frame, I capture the moment.The emotion, the feeling , something that will not just give a result, but tell a book's worth story. I don't like calling my self a photographer or videographer. I like to call my self a visual storyteller,because what i give in a story,an emotion like every book or tale.

My style of capturing something ,can be described as unique ,crazy , and artistic , i love giving a unique perspective of the moment to the final image, while making it look effortless.
